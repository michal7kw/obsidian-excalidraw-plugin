I've launched the Techno-Visual PKM Community. This is the recording of the public launch session. My vision for Techno-Visual PKM is a community of Visual Thinkers who like to play with scripting and automation in Obsidian.md to automate their PKM workflows. This will be a membership-based community for people who want to learn Dataview, Templater, Exclidraw Automate, Obsidian API, AI for Templater, Plugin development, and more.
