# Summary
In this video, the creator shares their process of writing and self-publishing a book for free using tools like Obsidian, Pandoc, and Calibre. They provide insights into their workflow for converting markdown notes into professional-looking PDFs, EPUBs, and audiobooks, emphasizing the importance of connectivity between ideas within Obsidian. The video serves as a guide for aspiring authors interested in self-publishing using accessible and cost-effective tools.

- **Workflow Overview**: The author’s process involves using Obsidian for text and Excalidraw for illustrations, followed by using Pandoc for converting markdown files into EPUBs and PDFs.
- **Connectivity of Ideas**: Maintaining links between various notes in Obsidian enriches the writing process, ensuring the book is integrated with the author's broader knowledge base.
- **Output Formatting**: Special formatting and document properties are used to create visually appealing outputs tailored for different publication formats like eBooks and printed versions.
