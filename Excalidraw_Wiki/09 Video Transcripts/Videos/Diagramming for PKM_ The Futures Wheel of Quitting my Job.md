# Summary
In this video, Zsolt introduces the Futures Wheel, a diagramming tool used to analyze the potential impacts of quitting a job. He discusses the primary, secondary, and tertiary consequences of such a decision, emphasizing both the challenges and opportunities that may arise. Lastly, he shares how to use these insights to create actionable plans for the future, which will be further elaborated in a follow-up video.

# Key Takeaways
- The Futures Wheel helps map out the consequences of major decisions, such as quitting a job, by categorizing impacts into primary, secondary, and tertiary levels.
- Quitting may lead to immediate financial instability and emotional relief, but it can also cause identity crises and loss of social status.
- Utilizing tools like GPT-4 can provide critical feedback on the Futures Wheel and help identify gaps in thinking, paving the way for more informed decision-making.
