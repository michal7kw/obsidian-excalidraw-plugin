# Summary
In this video, Zsolt discusses his journey of writing and self-publishing a book titled "Sketch Your Mind" in just six weeks. He shares his progress, including reaching a full draft of 55,000 words, as well as the tools and techniques he is utilizing to enhance the writing process, such as the Quiet Outline plugin for Obsidian. Zsolt also seeks audience input regarding his book cover designs and emphasizes the balance between quality and deadlines in his publishing journey.

# Key Takeaways
- Zsolt has completed his draft of "Sketch Your Mind," amounting to 55,000 words.
- He is leveraging various tools and plugins in Obsidian, including Quiet Outline and Pandoc, to improve his book's formatting and organization.
- Zsolt is experimenting with audio versions of his writing and is learning about Kindle digital publishing, including pre-orders and formatting challenges.
