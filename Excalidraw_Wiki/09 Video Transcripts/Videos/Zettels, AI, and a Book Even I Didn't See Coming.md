# Summary
In this video, Jolt shares insights gained from the recent PKM summit that transformed his perspective on personal knowledge management (PKM) tools. He discusses the challenges of Zettelkasten and how recognizing the "least versa" parent idea can enhance the creative process. Additionally, he emphasizes the risks associated with over-reliance on AI, particularly the potential loss of personal agency in decision-making and brainstorming.

# Key Takeaways
- The concept of Zettelkasten allows for linking ideas, but focusing on finding the "least versa" parent idea can foster unique personal connections in thoughts.
- Writing and organizing thoughts should emphasize chains of ideas rather than just pools of thoughts for better clarity and coherence.
- Over-dependence on AI tools can lead to a loss of personal initiative and contribute to a form of groupthink, highlighting the importance of maintaining one's agency in creative processes.
