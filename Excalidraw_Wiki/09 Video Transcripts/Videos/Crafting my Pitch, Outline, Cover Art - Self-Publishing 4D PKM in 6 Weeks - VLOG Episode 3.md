# Summary
In this video, Zsolt shares his progress on writing a book about visual personal knowledge management (PKM) within six weeks. He discusses his target audience, the unique angle of merging visual thinking with PKM, and the creative processes involved in developing the book’s outline and cover art. The video emphasizes the importance of community feedback and the use of technology like ChatGPT to enhance the writing process.

# Key Takeaways
- Zsolt identifies three target audiences for his book: PKM enthusiasts, visual creators, and aspiring visual thinkers who believe they cannot draw.
- The book aims to merge the worlds of personal knowledge management and visual thinking to create innovative approaches for distractions in today's fast-paced life.
- Feedback from the community is crucial, and Zsolt engages them through surveys and discussions about content and design choices for the book.
